About Us

The FIRST Group

We are a training and consulting company specializing in Fire, Incident Command, Rescue, Safety and Security Training.

What We Offer

We offer personalized, cost-effective solutions to meet our clients’ needs.

Experience

The FIRST Group and its network of professionals boasts numerous years of experience across a wide range of disciplines.

Training and Consulting

The FIRST Group LLC is a training and consulting company that was established in November 2012 by two professional Firemen, Matt Dupre and Greg Kelly.

Goal

Our Goal is to bring our real-world experience to the classroom. Simply put, we feel this is our responsibility to the emergency preparedness and response community.

Need

We recognize that no matter your discipline, job title, or experience level, it is imperative that you never stop learning.

Value

At The FIRST Group we value people and relationships. From our clients, to our staff, to our partners, we feel that everyone should be treated with respect and dignity. We appreciate and foster an environment that promotes esteem and professionalism.

Our Team

Our team is compromised of experienced response personnel who have dedicated their careers to being professional and knowledgeable. Our team members hail from many disciplines, such as NOFD, FDNY, NYPD, the U.S. military, private industry, and local and state emergency management entities.

Training the Trainers

Together we work towards providing our clients with outstanding services that exceed their expectations. In an effort to deliver the best services to our clients, our team is constantly seeking continuing education and professional development activities in order to keep up with the latest information, trends, procedures, and standards.
